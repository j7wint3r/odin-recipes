Egg fried rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a popular Chinese stir-fried rice dish made with cooked rice, scrambled eggs, soy sauce, and green onions.

It's a quick, versatile dish, often using leftover rice, where ingredients are tossed and fried in a wok or pan until well combined and heated through.

The eggs add flavor, color, and protein, while soy sauce and sesame oil provide seasoning and aroma, making it a simple yet satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 250g long grain rice
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 onion
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 spring onions

Steps

Step 1:
Cook the rice according to the package instructions, drain, spread it out to steam-dry, and set aside.
Step 2:
Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a large wok over high heat. Add the onion and fry for about 5 minutes until lightly browned.
Step 3:
Add the rice to the wok, stir, and toast for about 3 minutes. Move the rice to the side of the pan.
Step 4:
Add the remaining oil to the pan, then pour in the egg mixture.
Step 5:
Allow the eggs to cook for a bit, then mix them with the rice. Stir vigorously to coat the grains, or let the eggs set a little longer before breaking up and mixing through.
Step 6:
Transfer the rice to a serving bowl, scatter with spring onions, and optionally add sesame oil, ground white pepper, and a splash of soy sauce for seasoning.
